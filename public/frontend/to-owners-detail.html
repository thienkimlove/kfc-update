Drive Thru

Purchase of land for construction of stand-alone building with the area from 1,800 to 5,000 square m

Land plot selection criteria: - Intensive road traffic nearby the object. Additional pedestrian traffic is an advantage. - Immediate direct access from the main road. Availability of the entrance to/exit from the plot. - Location: thoroughfares, busy crossings, malls parking. - Perfect visibility of the object from the distance not less than 200 meters.

We will help you in all matters from the feasibility study till the personnel trainings
